About Us

Establishment: RGL Transient began its journey in March 2023 as a humble establishment, founded with a vision to provide exceptional transient accommodations for travelers seeking comfort and convenience.

Founders: The brainchild of Arlie Dela Cruz and Lito Lazaro, RGL Transient stands as a testament to their shared passion for hospitality and dedication to creating memorable experiences for guests from all walks of life.

Mission:

At RGL Transient, our mission is to redefine the transient accommodation experience by offering unparalleled comfort, personalized service, and a warm, welcoming environment that feels like home. We are committed to exceeding the expectations of our guests by providing exceptional service and amenities that cater to their every need.

Vision:

Our vision is to become the premier choice for transient accommodations, known for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability. We aspire to create a global network of RGL Transient properties that serve as beacons of hospitality, where every guest feels valued, respected, and truly at home. With a focus on continuous improvement and guest satisfaction, we aim to set new standards of excellence in the transient accommodation industry.
